About Antique Khan

Antique Khan is more than just a store — it’s a journey through history, art, and timeless craftsmanship. Founded by Marwan Mohammed Mahrous, a passionate antique collector and front-end developer, Antique Khan was born out of pure love for the stories behind every vintage piece.

Our Vision

To revive the beauty of the past and make authentic antiques accessible to everyone who values originality, elegance, and craftsmanship. Each item in our collection is handpicked with an eye for history and design.

Our Collection

From classic French décor to vintage glassware, bronze sculptures, and Sèvres vases, our collection blends European charm with Middle Eastern heritage — each piece tells a story worth keeping.

Why Choose Us

  • Authentic antiques curated with passion and expertise
  • Carefully restored and preserved with respect for history
  • Unique pieces that bring elegance and warmth to any home
  • Personal guidance for collectors and interior enthusiasts

Connect With Us

Follow us on our social media pages to explore new arrivals, rare finds, and behind-the-scenes stories from the antique world. We’re always happy to share knowledge and passion with fellow collectors.
